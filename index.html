Problem statement: Unbearable Gym Wait Times

The wait for different machines/equipment in the gym are too long. People are unable to finish their lifts due to the long wait times for certain machines/equipment or they are spending more time of their day at the gym due to waiting for equipment.

Affinity diagram: Gym Equipment

The Affinity Diagram is split into 5 clusters. Random, Startup Thoughts, Product Ideas, Partnerships, and Questions. The random section has ideas that don't fit into any other category. The startup thoughts are the main ideas and thoughts about how to get the company started and growing. Product ideas contain lots of different solutions for the problem, but most of the solutions involve technology. The partnership cluster is ideas about different partners and connections that could be beneficial to the growth of the company. The last cluster is just different questions that we need to try and find answers in order to create our product.
